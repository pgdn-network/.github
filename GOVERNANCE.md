# Governance (Lightweight)

PGDN OSS is maintained by a small group of maintainers and community contributors.

- **Decisions**: Made by maintainers after open discussion in Issues/PRs/Discord.
- **Release management**: Each repo’s maintainers tag releases; research publishes dated reports.
- **Disagreements**: We aim for consensus; maintainers have final say to keep projects moving.
- **Becoming a maintainer**: Consistent, high‑quality contributions and positive community participation, nominated by existing maintainers.

This document will evolve as the community grows.
